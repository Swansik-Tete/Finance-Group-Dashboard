# Financial-Group-Dashboard

## Overview
The Financial Group Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to monitor the company’s financial health and key business KPIs.  
The project focuses on financial analysis, cash flow tracking, profitability measurement, and budget performance using dynamic visualizations and filters.

---

## Objective
The main objective of this project is to provide a monthly-level financial dashboard that helps stakeholders monitor:

- Revenue performance
- Profitability trends
- Cash flow health
- Budget vs Actual performance
- Regional and product-wise financial analysis

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX
- Financial Analysis
- Data Visualization

## Key Insights
- Total revenue of **23.6M** shows a stable monthly trend with minor fluctuations, indicating consistent business performance over time.
- Revenue distribution is fairly balanced across regions, with North and East contributing slightly higher, reducing geographic dependency risk.
- Budget variance of **-24.23%** suggests underperformance against targets.
- Product mix is evenly distributed (~20% each category), indicating diversification of revenue.
- Gross profit margin of **45.55%** and EBITDA of **24.81%** indicate good profitability.
- Total expenses (**COGS: 12.86M, Opex: 5M**) form a significant portion of revenue, highlighting cost structure as a key profitability driver.
- Operating expenses are consistently **~25–27%** of COGS across all regions.
- Cash flow remains positive (**Net Cash Flow: 688K**), indicating good liquidity.
- East region and Product A / Service Y contribute positively to cash flow changes, while South, West region and Product X, B, C show negative impact.

## Assumptions
- The dataset is assumed to be clean, complete, and accurate.
- Financial metrics are assumed to be consistently defined and calculated.
- All transactions are considered valid and free from anomalies.
- Currency values are assumed uniform with no inflation or conversion adjustments.
- Time trends are assumed to reflect actual business performance.
